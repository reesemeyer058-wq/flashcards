Print

Print steps

  1. Click Print fronts → print on normal letter paper.
  2. Put the same paper back in your printer.
  3. Click Print backs.
  • Scale: 100% (turn OFF “Fit to page”).
  • These print as outlined 3×5 boxes so you can cut them out.

Fix back-side alignment (if needed)

Use Print test sheet first to figure out the right settings for your printer.

Mass import
